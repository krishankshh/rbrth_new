The Mission

RBRTH was founded on a simple realization: the supplement industry is broken. It's filled with complex chemicals, sugar-laden fillers, and promises that don't hold up under scientific scrutiny.

We started with Spear Mints because we believe that performance starts in the brain. By combining instant freshness with essential electrolytes, we've created a product that supports both mental clarity and physiological recovery.

Pure Engineering

Our process is purely data-driven. From the fermentation process used to create natural sweetness to the precise electrolyte balance in every mint, we engineer our products for those who demand the most from their bodies and minds.

The Why

Because "good enough" is the enemy of greatness. We build for the 1% who refuse to settle for mediocre nutrition.

The How

By leveraging biotechnology and clean fermentation to bypass the need for artificial sweeteners and palm oil.
